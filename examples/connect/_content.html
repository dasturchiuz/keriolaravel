Part of our Kerio Connect mission is to build an open communication and collaboration platform. Not only "open" in terms of supporting many protocols, standards, clients, and platforms. But also "open" in allowing administrators and developers to easily access the product to integrate it with third party solutions. This means the ability to write scripts and tools that simplify their work, or customize Kerio Connect to better fit their environment.

The Administration API for Kerio Connect allows administrators to facilitate their daily work and automate many procedures.
